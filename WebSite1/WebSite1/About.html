Our Purpose

JavaScript 1, 2, 3! is a non-profit organisation dedicated to bring JavaScript to the masses.

We have developed a 3 step system that makes learning easy. We believe in the see one, do one, teach one method.

This involves the learner observing how a technique is used. Practicing it themselves and then going on to teach someone else what they have learnt.
